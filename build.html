Our Robot's Manual

When we were first designing our robot, we wanted to make sure that all the concepts that we were thinking of could be incorporated into one robot. In order to do this, we decided to first make a digital design of the robot we wanted. After carefully selecting all the parts and putting them together, this was our final design that is now made out of LEGO and used for our robot runs. Along with the digital design, we made an instruction manual on how to build our robot.
