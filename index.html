We are a co-ed FLL robotics team from Bayview Glen School in Toronto, Ontario, Canada consisting of five energetic students from grade 5 to grade 10. Our team members collaborate well together, both inside and outside FLL, in all that we do. We love sharing our passion and knowledge about the research project and the robot to other teams and to the general public. This is all made possible by our generous sponsors and dedicated mentors.
