How we can make learning rhythm, lolishermusic notation, and pitch recognition easier?

Using the Rhythm Ramp!

The Rhythm Ramp is a ramp that a ball rolls down to create a melody by hitting chimes. The gaps in between the chimes represent a rest or the duration of the note. The Rhythm Ramp will also include a booklet that first guides the children in assembling melodies. As the student progress, it will allow children to self-test on how to create melodies from music notation.
